About Us

OPUS BOTICS is a startup started by the students of national colleges which conducts workshops on robotics. Our aim is making INDIA very good at robotics. We all know that robotics and automation are the two big things coming up. So, we wanted to spread this robotics all over the country, which helps INDIA's growth in technology and advancement. By our workshops we make robotics very easy and entertaining.
